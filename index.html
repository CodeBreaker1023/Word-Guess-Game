GAME PLAY: The name of the game is to guess the word displayed below before the man belows meets his fate by hanging. Choose from the list of letters. Correctly chosen letters will appear in their appropriate spaces. If you guess incorrectly, the condemned man will advance one step closer to death. Guessing the word in its entirety is the only way to save him. The amount of men you save or put to death will be tallied in the corner. Hit the "Start Over" button to clear the board and get a new word.
